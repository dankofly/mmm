# MMM - Money Making Machine

AI-powered prompt chains and systems for generating revenue, validating business ideas, and creating viral content.

Based on proven frameworks from Sabrina Ramonov, Alex Hormozi, and the Council methodology.

## Revenue Systems

### 1. Money Prompt Chain (`/chains/money-chain/`)
5-prompt chain: "I have skills" → "I have paying customers" in one conversation.

### 2. High-Ticket Offer Architect (`/chains/high-ticket-offer/`)
6-stage AI interview that builds a $3K-$10K offer from your life experience.

### 3. AI Co-Founder MEGA Prompt (`/chains/ai-cofounder/`)
Delete 99% of your to-do list. Find the 3 moves that multiply growth. Mixture of Experts technique.

### 4. 8 Income Streams (`/chains/income-streams/`)
Proven income streams you can build while employed. From AI training ($290/h) to micro apps ($100K/mo).

### 5. Business Audit System (`/chains/business-audit/`)
Evidence-based idea validation. 15-criteria scoring, CENTS/Hormozi/Thiel tests, Pre-Mortem.

## Content & Distribution

### 6. Viral Slides System (`/chains/viral-slides/`)
NotebookLM presentations in 5 minutes. 14 viral layouts, 3 tiers, 8-point content structure.

### 7. Humanize AI Writing (`/chains/humanize-writing/`)
The definitive prompt to make AI writing sound human. Banned words, style rules, voice matching.

### 8. AI Sparring Partner (`/chains/ai-sparring-partner/`)
Transform AI from yes-man to critical thinking partner. Find blind spots, not validation.

### 9. AI Social Media System (`/chains/ai-social-media-system/`)
Fully automated: article → platform-specific posts across all social media. Plus AI Avatar Clone system.

### 10. Claude + Remotion Videos (`/chains/remotion-videos/`)
5 video templates: explainers, product demos, testimonials, avatar overlays, data dashboards.

## Learning

### 11. Claude Code Full Course (`/chains/claude-code-course/`)
6-step path to building an AI Marketing Officer. Plus 30 concepts explained for normal people.

## Quick Start

1. Pick a system from `/chains/`
2. Read the `prompts.md` in that folder
3. Copy prompts into your AI tool (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)
4. Follow the chain sequence — don't skip steps
