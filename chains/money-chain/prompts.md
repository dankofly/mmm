# Money Prompt Chain — Complete Prompts

## PREREQUISITE: Establish Context

Before running any prompts, give the AI context about yourself. Use this template:

```
I'm a [Your Background]. My skills include [Skill 1, Skill 2]. I'm passionate about [Interest]. I have [Time Available] per week and a beginner-level understanding of [Relevant Field]. My goal is to build an income stream that [Vague Goal: helps small businesses, lets me work from home, helps improve health, etc].
```

---

## PROMPT 1 — Identify Offer + Channel

```
Based on everything you know about me, what is the fastest way for me to make [INCOME GOAL]? Limit to 1 offer and 1 channel.
```

### What to do after:
- Do NOT accept the answer passively
- Have a constructive dialogue back and forth
- Ask clarifying questions
- Push back on the recommendation
- Propose alternatives and ask why the AI chose its recommendation over yours
- Move to Prompt 2 once you're happy with the offer and channel

---

## PROMPT 2 — Identify Mentor

```
Who is the #1 person I should learn from to succeed?
```

### What to do after:
- Stay in the same conversation
- This answer feeds directly into Prompt 3

---

## PROMPT 3 — Create Action Plan (as the Mentor)

```
Pretend you are that person. Create a VERY SIMPLE 90-day action plan that needs <$1,000 upfront, has a 95% chance of success, and gets me to my FIRST paying customer ASAP.
```

### Critical note:
The phrase "gets me to my FIRST paying customer ASAP" is the key constraint. Without it, the AI will generate a typical 30/60/90 day roadmap where the first 30 days is spent on "preparation."

The first dollar builds momentum. Most people quit too early or jump to the next shiny object. Compounding small wins early is critical.

---

## PROMPT 4 — Validate the Offer (Intermediate)

> "Your first 10 customers are the only market research that matters. What others did is a hypothesis. What you can get someone to pay for is the truth."

```
Act as Alex Hormozi. I have no audience or distribution. My goal is to get one "hell yes" or paid commitment ASAP. What are 3 specific, easy-to-run tests I can do this week to validate that real people will pay me? Each test should cost under $50 and <4 hours to set up.

My preferences: [insert your preferences — e.g., no face on camera, text-based outreach only]

My constraints: [insert your constraints — e.g., no budget for paid ads, limited to evenings]
```

### Customization examples for constraints:
- You don't want to show your face on social media
- You don't want to talk about certain topics
- You don't have budget for paid ads
- You only have evenings/weekends

---

## PROMPT 5 — Diagnose Mindset Block (Advanced)

> "Oftentimes, the roadmap to success is clear, straightforward, and proven by many others. However, people tend to quit way too early."

```
I'm stuck at the starting line. Acting as my entrepreneur coach, ask me questions one at a time, until you can successfully diagnose the ONE mindset block most likely holding me back. Then reframe it. Finally, compress my 90-day plan into a 2-week "proof sprint" that proves I can succeed.
```

### How this works:
- This prompt INVERTS the dynamic — instead of you asking questions, the AI asks YOU questions
- Answer honestly — the AI will diagnose your specific psychological obstacle
- The "proof sprint" compresses your 90-day plan into 2 weeks of focused action
- This is designed to break through analysis paralysis and imposter syndrome
