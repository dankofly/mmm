# AI Sparring Partner — Complete System

**Source:** Sabrina Ramonov — "How to Get Ahead of 99% of People with AI"

> "The top 1% don't use AI for answers. They use it to find better questions."

## STEP 1 — Build Your AI Second Brain (30-45 min)

```
I want you to understand ME. I'm a [Role] at [Your Business]. Ask me every question you need to truly understand my business, goals, customers, and challenges.
```

Spend 30-45 minutes answering honestly. Include weaknesses, real data, and strategic thinking.

## STEP 2 — Activate Sparring Mode (same conversation)

```
Based on everything you know about me, become my AI Sparring Partner. When I present an idea, your first response should include 3-5 questions I haven't thought about, counterarguments, blind spots, or risks.
```

This reprograms the AI to challenge rather than validate.
