# AI Co-Founder MEGA Prompt

Stop doing too much. Find your 3 highest-leverage moves.

**Source:** Sabrina Ramonov — "If you want 2026 to be the best year of your life"
**Time:** 1-4 hours (deep work session)
**Works with:** ChatGPT, Gemini, DeepSeek, Claude

## The Problem

Most entrepreneurs fail because they're doing too much. 10 goals, 237 tasks, all at once. In the pursuit of ALL, you end up with NOTHING.

## The Solution

A mega prompt that forces AI to:
- Analyze your hard numbers
- Identify your distractions
- Ask YOU questions until it determines your top 3 highest-leverage priorities

> "A real co-founder doesn't add work. They subtract it."

## 4-Part Structure

```
Part 1: ROLE — Prime AI as top 0.1% founder in your industry
        |
Part 2: CONTEXT — Product, customers, goals, 5-year vision
        |
Part 3: DATA — Real revenue, churn, funnel, expenses (don't lie)
        |
Part 4: ACTIVITIES — 2025 results + full 2026 to-do dump
        |
TASK: Find the TOP 3 things that multiply growth
        |
MAGIC LINE: "Ask me clarifying questions, one at a time,
             until you are 95% confident in your answer."
```

## The Magic Line

The last instruction is everything:

> "Ask me clarifying questions, one at a time, until you are 95% confident in your answer."

This forces iterative interrogation. The AI will roast you with questions. At any point, ask it to reveal its confidence score.

## Advanced: Mixture of Experts

Run the same mega prompt on 3+ LLMs simultaneously:
- **Gemini** — diverges into one rabbit hole
- **ChatGPT** — explores different angles
- **DeepSeek** — yet another perspective

Cross-reference their conclusions. Copy-paste one AI's answer into another and ask what it thinks. You get interrogated from multiple angles.

## Results

Sabrina's results from a single 4-hour flight:
- Deleted 237 tasks
- Found exactly 3 high-leverage priorities
- Full clarity on her annual plan
