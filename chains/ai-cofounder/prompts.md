## HOW TO FILL IT IN

### Part 1: Role
Change "tech founder" to match your industry:
- SaaS → "tech founder"
- Real Estate → "real estate mogul"
- E-commerce → "e-commerce operator"
- Agency → "agency founder"
- Creator → "creator economy founder"

### Part 2: Context
- **Product:** What you sell, link if possible
- **Highest retention group:** Which customers stick? This is your real market.
- **2026 goal:** Specific revenue or growth target
- **Vision:** Where the company is in 5 years

### Part 3: Data (DON'T LIE)
- Real MRR, churn rate, ARPU
- Funnel: visitor → trial → paid conversion rates
- Expenses by category (salary, tools, marketing, contractors, fees)

### Part 4: Activities
- **What worked in 2025:** Marketing channels that drove actual ROI
- **2026 to-do dump:** Copy-paste your ENTIRE overwhelming list. Literally everything.

## ADVANCED: MIXTURE OF EXPERTS

After running the mega prompt on one AI, do this:

1. **Open 3 AI tabs:** Gemini, ChatGPT, DeepSeek (or Claude)
2. **Paste the same mega prompt** in each
3. **Answer each AI's questions separately** — they'll diverge into different rabbit holes
4. **Cross-reference:** When one AI gives a final answer, copy it into another and ask: "What do you think of this strategy? What's missing?"
5. **Triangulate:** The overlap between all 3 is your highest-confidence answer

### Why it works:
- Each AI has different training data and reasoning patterns
- You get interrogated from multiple angles
- Conflicting recommendations force deeper thinking
- The consensus items are the real priorities
