# 8 Income Streams While Employed

**Source:** Sabrina Ramonov — "8 Income Streams You Can Build While STILL an Employee"

## 1. Train AI Models With Your Expertise

Companies pay specialists to label data and train AI models.

**Rates (via Mercor):**
- Radiologists: $290-$340/hour
- Software Engineers: $70-$120/hour
- Lawyers: up to $150/hour
- Sales & Management: $90-$150/hour

**Action:** Apply on platforms like Mercor with your domain credentials.

---

## 2. Build Your Personal Brand

Create content on social platforms, monetize through sponsorships.

**Income:**
- Nano (<10K followers): $500-$2,000/post
- Macro: $20,000-$45,000/post

**Action:** Pick niche, post consistently evenings/weekends, build to 10K+, pitch brands.

---

## 3. Build a Faceless Brand

Video content without showing your face. Can be outsourced and sold.

**Action:** Identify trending faceless formats, use AI avatars or screen recordings, optimize for algorithms.

---

## 4. Repurpose Content for Big Creators

Manage cross-platform expansion for creators who only post on one platform.

**Pitch:**
```
I saw you're crushing it on [Platform] but invisible on [Other Platform]. Let me own [Other Platform] for you, you won't have to do anything.
```

**Action:** Target creators with 50K+ followers on one platform, propose handling repurposing.

---

## 5. Coach People on Claude Code

One-on-one guidance helping people learn and implement Claude Code.

**Action:** Spend 2 weeks learning Claude Code, create TikTok/IG demos, offer coaching via link-in-bio.

---

## 6. AI Consulting

Implement AI solutions for businesses. Lower effort variant: AI training (teaching teams).

**Action:** Document expertise, create case studies, market via LinkedIn.

---

## 7. TikTok Shop

Sell products via TikTok Shop while learning creator fundamentals.

**Skills developed:** Content creation, hooks, A/B testing, viral writing, volume production.

---

## 8. Vibe Code Micro Apps

Build simple single-feature AI apps. Keep it stupid simple. 99% effort on marketing.

**Example:** Pet photo to Renaissance portrait = $100K/month.

**Action:**
1. Find a simple, repeatable user problem
2. Build one-step flow: input -> AI -> output
3. Invest heavily in marketing
4. Scale or launch new micro apps

---

## Author's Pick: Personal Brand

Higher leverage, free to start, compounds over time. Pick one stream, execute nights and weekends.
