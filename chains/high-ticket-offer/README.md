# High-Ticket Offer Architect

AI-guided interview that extracts your life experience and builds a $3K-$10K digital offer from it.

**Format:** 6-stage structured interview — the AI asks YOU questions
**Time:** ~30-45 minutes for the full interview
**Output:** Complete offer architecture with positioning, pricing, and messaging

## How It Works

The AI interviews you through 6 stages in sequence. It will NOT skip ahead or give advice until each stage is complete.

```
Stage 1: Background Discovery
  "What shaped you? What have you overcome?"
        |
        v
Stage 2: Skill Extraction
  "What can you teach? What results have you achieved?"
        |
        v
Stage 3: Market Alignment
  "Who would pay for this? Which industry/persona?"
        |
        v
Stage 4: Offer Architecture
  "What format? What result? How long?"
        |
        v
Stage 5: High-Ticket Validation
  "Is the transformation worth $3K-$10K? What's the cost of inaction?"
        |
        v
Stage 6: Positioning & Messaging
  "What's your hook? Your story angle? Your core message?"
```

## What You Get

After the 6 stages:
- A clearly defined offer with format, duration, and deliverables
- A validated price point ($3K-$10K) backed by ROI logic
- Your unique positioning hook and story angle
- Core messaging that attracts the right audience
- Emotional AND logical buying arguments

## Usage

1. Copy the prompt from `prompts.md` into a new AI conversation
2. Answer honestly and in detail — the more you give, the better the offer
3. Don't rush — each stage builds on the previous one
4. The AI will tell you when to move to the next stage
